## üöÄ Quick Technical Summary

TALOS addresses the $150B/year cloud waste problem through an autonomous **OODA Loop (Observe-Orient-Decide-Act)**. Unlike traditional tools that merely provide dashboards, TALOS **acts** on its findings using a **tiered AI Swarm** that balances cost-efficiency with high-stakes reasoning.

* **Stack**: Go (Golang), PostgreSQL, Redis, React, OpenTelemetry.
* **AI Engine**: Tiered integration with Gemini 1.5 (Pro/Flash), Claude 3.5 Sonnet, GPT-4o, and Devin.
* **Architecture**: Distributed worker model with idempotent state management and "Guardian" safety protocols.

## ‚ú® Key Innovation Points

1. **Tiered Intelligence (The Swarm)**: Uses cheap LLMs for monitoring (Sentinel) and high-reasoning LLMs (Arbiter/Oracle) only for high-risk decisions, achieving 100x+ ROI on AI tokens.

3. **Double-Loop Learning**: The system records its decisions in a `ProjectMemory`, allowing it to learn from previous infrastructure changes and avoid repeating sub-optimal optimizations.
4. **Indie-Force & Enterprise Modes**: Versatile enough for solo founders (aggressive cost cutting) and enterprises (safe, audited optimizations).
